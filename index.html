What Are CLB Levels?

CLB (Canadian Language Benchmarks) measures your French skills and helps you understand your level for immigration and exams:

  • CLB 1–3 (A1) Basic phrases, introductions, simple questions
  • CLB 4–5 (A2) Daily situations, simple conversations, short texts
  • CLB 6–7 (B1) Talk about experiences, understand main points, handle travel/work situations
  • CLB 8–9 (B2) Express opinions, follow complex texts, communicate confidently
  • CLB 10–11 (C1) Fluent, professional/academic language, detailed comprehension
  • CLB 12 (C2) Mastery, precise and effortless in any situation

Important: You don't have to be the same level in every skill. You could be B1 in writing but B2 or even C2 in speaking, reading, or listening. Hitena helps you strengthen each skill individually so you can maximize your points and confidence.
